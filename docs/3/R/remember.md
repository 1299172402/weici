英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **记得；记住；想起：**  

 > I remember my first day at kindergarten very well / clearly.  
 > 我清楚地记得第一天上幼儿园的情景。    
<audio src="./media/I remember my first day _AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I can’t remember his telephone number.  
 > 我想不起他的电话号码了。    
<audio src="./media/I can’t remember his telephone number_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- remember doing sth 记得曾做过某事：

 > I remember meeting her at a party once.   
 > 我记得曾在一次聚会上见过她。    
<audio src="./media/remember-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- remember to do sth 记着要做某事：

 > Remember to turn off the light before you leave.   
 > 走前别忘了关灯。    
<audio src="./media/remember-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- remember + (that) / wh- 从句：

 > I remember that there was a beautiful valley near the village.   
 > 我记得村庄附近有个漂亮的山谷。  （外研③ – 6）  
<audio src="./media/remember-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Do you remember what you said last night?   
 > 你记得自己昨晚说过的话吗？  （人教⑧ – 4）  
<audio src="./media/remember-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*低义频：* **纪念：**  

 > the custom of remembering Jesus’ birth   
 > 纪念耶稣诞生的习俗  （人教③ – 1）  

### 3.*低义频：* **代...致意；代...问好：**  

 > Will you kindly **remember me to** your family?   
 > 请代我向你的家人问好，好吗?    
<audio src="./media/remember-9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


固定搭配
---
## \#be remembered as / for 
1.**因…而著名：**  

 > Madame Curie will always be remembered as the discoverer of the element radium.   
 > 居里夫人作为镭元素的发现者将永远被人们记住。    
<audio src="./media/remember-10.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Margaret Mitchell is best remembered for her book, Gone with the Wind.   
 > 玛格丽特·米歇尔因其著作《飘》而著名。
