## \#rely on / upon
1.*高义频：* **依靠**  
英译 to depend on sb / sth：

 > You shouldn’t always **rely** just **on** the Internet **for** your research.  
 > 你不能总是依靠网络进行研究。  （2015 湖南）  
<audio src="./media/You shouldn’t always rely just on_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- rely on / upon … to do sth:

 > These days, we rely heavily on computers to organize our work.  
 > 现在，我们在很大程度上依赖电脑来安排工作。    
<audio src="./media/rely52.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

2.*高义频：* **信任**  
英译 to trust or have faith in sb / sth：

- rely on / upon … to do sth:

 > I can rely on David to keep my secret.  
 > 我信任大卫，他一定会为我保密。
