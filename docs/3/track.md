## \#keep track of 
1.**了解…的动态；与…保持联系：**  

 > They read the newspapers every day to keep track of current events.   
 > 他们每天阅读报纸，以了解时事。

## \#lose track of 
1.**失去…的线索；与…失去联系；忘记：**  

 > The police have lost track of the criminals.   
 > 警方已失去罪犯的线索。
