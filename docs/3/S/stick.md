英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **(被) 刺入；(被) 戳进：**  

 > The nurse **stuck** the needle **into** my arm.  
 > 护士把针扎进我的手臂。    
<audio src="./media/stick-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I found a nail **sticking in** the tyre.  
 > 我发现轮胎上扎了一根钉子。    
<audio src="./media/stick-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **粘 (住)；贴 (住)：**  

 > stick a stamp on an envelope  
 > 在信封上贴邮票    

 > This food can **stick to** your teeth.  
 > 这种食物容易粘牙。    
<audio src="./media/stick-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*低义频：* **伸出：**  

 > Peter stuck his head behind the door.  
 > 彼得从门后探出头来。    
<audio src="./media/stick-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*高义频：* **[非正式用语] 放置：**  

 > Stick your bags down there.  
 > 把你们的包搁在那儿吧。    
<audio src="./media/stick-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 5.*高义频：* **阻塞；(被) 难住：**  

 > The key has stuck in the lock.  
 > 钥匙卡在锁里了。    
<audio src="./media/stick-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > He was stuck by this question.  
 > 他被这个问题难住了。    
<audio src="./media/stick-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


固定搭配
---
## \#be / get stuck in
1.**卡住；陷入：**  

 > They got stuck in a traffic jam.  
 > 他们遇到了交通堵塞。    
<audio src="./media/stick-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#stick around
1.**[非正式用语] 不走开；待在原地**  

## \#stick out
1.**伸出；突出：**  

 > Stick out your tongue, please.  
 > 请把舌头伸出来。    
<audio src="./media/stick-9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The photo showed Edward with very short hair and his ears sticking out.  
 > 照片上，爱德华头发很短，双耳突出。    
<audio src="./media/stick-10.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#stick to
1.**坚持；遵守：**  

 > The surest way to success is sticking to your goal.  
 > 通向成功最可靠的方法是坚持目标。    
<audio src="./media/stick-11.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Once he made a promise, he would stick to it (= he did as he had promised).  
 > 他一旦许下诺言，就会坚守这个诺言。    
<audio src="./media/stick-12.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#stick up
1.**竖立：**  

 > On Christmas morning we stuck up a board with “A Merry Christmas” on it.  
 > 在圣诞节的早上，我们竖起一块牌子，上面写着“圣诞快乐”。  （北师大⑧ – 24）  
<audio src="./media/stick-13.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#stick with
1.*低义频：* **持续；坚持：**  

 > You should choose an activity that you enjoy, and one that you’ll stick with.  
 > 你应该选择一项你喜欢并且会坚持下去的活动。  （2009 湖南改）  
<audio src="./media/stick-14.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

2.*低义频：* **留在...的记忆里：**  

 > Those words will stick with me for the rest of my life.  
 > 我余生将永远记住这些话。    
<audio src="./media/stick-15.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. The cards had been ________ (卡住) through the letterbox.  （2011 全国）  
2. Once a decision has been made, all of us should stick _______ it.  （2004 湖北改）  

答案：
1. stuck  2. to
