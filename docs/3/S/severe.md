英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **严重的**  
英译 extremely bad or serious：

 > severe damage / a severe crisis   
 > 严重的损害/危机    

### 2.*高义频：* **严厉的；苛刻的：**  

 > be severe with oneself  
 > 严于律己    

### 3.*低义频：* **严峻的；激烈的：**  

 > the severe winter  
 > 严冬    

 > severe competition  
 > 激烈的竞争

---
severely /sə'vɪrlɪ/ adv   
 > a severely polluted lake  
 > 严重污染的湖    

 > Anyone breaking the law will be severely punished.  
 > 任何人犯法都会受到严惩。
