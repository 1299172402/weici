固定搭配
---
## \#such as
1.**诸如；例如：**  

 > Cartoon characters such as Mickey Mouse and Snoopy are still popular.  
 > 像米老鼠和史努比这样的卡通人物仍然很受欢迎。    
<audio src="./media/such-12.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: such as常用来进行列举，并直接放在列举的名 词之前。for example表示“例如”，用来举例说明某一情况或论点，可放在句首、句中或句末。  
 > She does well in many subjects such as mathematics, physics, and chemistry.  
 > 她许多学科的成绩都很好，比如数学、物理和化学。    
<audio src="./media/such-13.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > His spelling is terrible! For example, look at this word.  
 > 他的拼写太糟糕了，例如，就看看这个单词吧。    
<audio src="./media/such-14.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Mexico, Canada, and China, for example, all have oil reserves.  
 > 例如墨西哥、加拿大和中国，都有石油储备。    
<audio src="./media/such-15.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. ________ is the power of TV that it can make a person suddenly famous.  （2009 辽宁）  
A.Such  
B.This  
C.That  
D.So  

答案：
1. A

英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **[无最高级和比较级] 这样的；那样的；此类的：**  

 > Such behaviour is just not acceptable in our school.  
 > 这样的行为在我们学校是绝对不能接受的。    
<audio src="./media/such-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#such..as
1.
 > **There is no such thing as** a free lunch.  
 > 世界上没有免费的午餐。    
<audio src="./media/such-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Walt Disney is credited for creating such wonderful things as Donald Duck and Mickey Mouse.  
 > 沃尔特·迪斯尼为人称赞是因为创造了诸如唐老鸭和米老鼠这样的美好事物。  （2014 福建）  
<audio src="./media/such-Walt Disney is credited.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **[用于强调] 那么的；如此的：**  

 > Why are you in such a hurry?   
 > 你为什么这么着急？    
<audio src="./media/such-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


固定搭配
---
## \#such … that 
1.**如此…以至于：**  

 > This issue is of such importance that we can’t afford to ignore it.  
 > 这个问题十分重要，我们疏忽不得。    
<audio src="./media/such-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > They said it was such a beautiful day that they would remember it forever  
 > 他们说那天如此美好，他们永远也不会忘记。  （2009 浙江）  
<audio src="./media/such-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#such that / as to 
1.**到如此程度以致：**  

 > The force of the explosion was such that windows were blown out.  
 > 爆炸的力量如此之大，以致窗户都被震碎了。    
<audio src="./media/such-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The pain in my foot wasn’t such as to stop my walking.  
 > 我脚疼，但还不至于不能行走。    
<audio src="./media/such-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: 与不定冠词连用时，such要放在不定冠词之前；与no, all, many, one, few, several, some, any等连用时，则放在其后。  
 > such a proposal   
 > 那样的建议    

 > I made no such decision.   
 > 我没有作过那样的决定。    
<audio src="./media/such-9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. The Great Wall is ________tourist attraction that millions of people pour in every year.  （2009上海）  
A.so a well-known  
B.a so well-known  
C.such well-known a  
D.such a well-known  
2. My English teacher’s humor was ________ make every student burst into laughter.  （2008 江西）  
A.so as to  
B.such as to  
C.such that  
D.so that  
3. His plan was such a good one ________ we all agreed to accept it.   
4. As I know, there is ________ car in this neighborhood.  
A.no such  
B.no a  
C.not such  
D.no such a  

答案：
1. D  2. B  3. that  4. A
