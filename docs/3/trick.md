英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **诡计；花招；恶作剧：**  

 > Her tears were just a trick to deceive others.  
 > 她的眼泪只是骗人的诡计。    
<audio src="./media/trick3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > If the neighbours do not give any sweets, the children might **play a trick on** them.  
 > 如果邻居们不给糖果，孩子们就可以捉弄他们。  （人教③ – 1）  
<audio src="./media/trick4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **戏法；把戏：**  

 > **perform magic tricks**  
 > 表演魔术    

### 3.*低义频：* **技巧；诀窍：**  
英译 a way of doing sth that works well; a good method：

 > Patience is **the trick of** doing this job well.  
 > 耐心是做好这份工作的诀窍。

真题解析
---
1. — Could you tell me the ________ (诀窍)of making such tasty cakes?
— Well, I just follow the directions in the cookbook.  （2014 福建改）  
A.feature  
B.plan  
C.cost  
D.trick  

答案：
1. trick
