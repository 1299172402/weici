真题解析
---
1. — Jack, you seem excited.
— ________? I won the first prize in the English speech contest.  （2015 天津）  
A.Guess what  
B.So what  
C.Pardon me  
D.Who cares  
2. — The boys are not doing a good job at all, are they?
— ________ .  
A.I guess not so  
B.I don’t guess  
C.I don’t guess so  
D.I guess not  

答案：
1. A  2. D
