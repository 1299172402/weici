## \#in relation to 
1.**[正式用语] 关于；与…相比：**  

 > I have a lot to say in relation to this affair.   
 > 关于这件事，我有很多话要说。    
<audio src="./media/relation-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Women’s earnings are still low in relation to men’s.  
 > 女性的收入相对于男性而言还是偏低。
