英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **坚持；坚决要求**  
英译 to demand that sth should happen：

 > — Why don't you call them up today?  
 > 为什么不今天就给他们电话呢？    
<audio src="./media/insist-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — OK, **if you insist**.   
 > 好，如果一定要我这样办的话。    

- insist on / upon (doing) sth:

 > I insist on an immediate answer.  
 > 我坚决要求立即答复。    
<audio src="./media/insist-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Tu Youyou and her team members even insisted on testing the medicine on themselves to make sure that it was safe.  
 > 屠呦呦和她的队友们甚至坚持在自己身上试验这种药，以确保它是安全的。  （人教新选必① – 1）  
<audio src="./media/Tu Youyou and her team members even insisted on testing the medicine on themselves to2_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- insist + (that) … (should) do sth:

 > He insisted (that) the problem (should) be discussed at the meeting.  
 > 他执意要求在会上讨论这个问题。  （2010 湖北改）  
<audio src="./media/insist-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **坚持说；坚持认为**  
英译 to say firmly that sth is true, especially when others don’t believe you：

- insist on sth:

 > He insisted on his innocence.   
 > 他坚持说自己是无辜的。    
<audio src="./media/insist-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- insist + (that) 从句：

 > He insisted that he was right.   
 > 他坚持认为自己是对的。    
<audio src="./media/insist-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: insist表示“坚持”和“坚决要求”时，that从句中用虚拟语气；而表示“坚持说”和“坚持认为”时则不用。  

真题解析
---
1. The man insisted on________ (find) a taxi for me even though I told him I lived nearby.
  （2004 江苏改）  

答案：
1. finding
