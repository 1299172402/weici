固定搭配
---
## \#burst in on / upon
1.**突然闯入而打断：**  

 > burst in on a meeting  
 > 突然闯入打断会议    

## \#burst into 
1.**突然迸发：**  

 > They **burst into tears / laughter / cheers**.  
 > 他们突然大哭/大笑/欢呼起来。    
<audio src="./media/6-burst.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#burst out
1.**突然大声喊叫；突然……起来：**  

 > burst out crying / laughing / cheering   
 > 突然大哭/大笑/欢呼起来
