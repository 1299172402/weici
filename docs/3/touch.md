英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **(伸手) 触摸；接触 (到)：**  

 > Don’t touch that dish — it’s very hot!   
 > 别摸那个盘子，它烫极了!    
<audio src="./media/touch-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > As our glasses touched, he said, “Cheers!”   
 > 我们碰杯，他说：“干杯！”    
<audio src="./media/touch-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Her dress was so long that it was touching the ground.   
 > 她的裙子太长，都拖地了。    
<audio src="./media/touch-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **触动；感动：**  

 > What he said really **touched** my **heart**.   
 > 他的话确实触动了我的心弦。    
<audio src="./media/touch-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*低义频：* **[常与否定词连用] 吃；喝；用 to eat, drink or use sth：**  

 > You have hardly touched your food.   
 > 你没怎么吃东西啊。    
<audio src="./media/touch-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > He hasn’t touched the money his aunt left him.   
 > 他还没动过姑妈给他的钱。    
<audio src="./media/touch-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*低义频：* **(说话或写作中) 简略提到；涉及：**  

 > The article touches many of the issues which are currently popular.  
 > 这篇文章涉及到了很多时下热门的话题。    
<audio src="./media/touch-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **触觉：**  

 > the sense of touch   
 > 触觉    

### 2.*高义频：* **触摸；碰；接触：**  

 > A bubble will burst at the slightest touch.   
 > 气泡稍微一碰就破。    
<audio src="./media/touch-10.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*低义频：* **手法；风格；技巧**  
英译 a way or style of doing sth：

 > The room was decorated with a very artistic touch.  
 > 房间装饰得很有艺术韵味。    
<audio src="./media/touch-11.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > This meal is awful. I think I’m **losing** my **touch** (= lost my ability).   
 > 这顿饭太难吃了。我想我的烹调水平下降了。    
<audio src="./media/touch-12.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


固定搭配
---
## \#(be) in touch (with) 
1.**(与…) 有联系：**  

 > Are you still in touch with your former colleagues?  
 > 你与以前的同事还有联系吗？    
<audio src="./media/touch-13.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#(be) out of touch (with) 
1.**(与…) 不联系 / 不接触：**  

 > We have been out of touch with them for about two years.   
 > 我们大约有两年没有和他们联系了。    
<audio src="./media/touch-14.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#get in touch (with) 
1.**(与…) 取得联系：**  

 > Here’s our number if you need to get in touch with us.   
 > 如果你需要联系我们，这是我们的号码。    
<audio src="./media/touch-16.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#keep / stay in touch (with)
1.**(与…) 保持联系：**  

 > Keeping in touch with our friends is an important part of friendship.   
 > 和朋友们保持联系在友谊中很重要。  （2012 湖南）  
<audio src="./media/touch-15.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#lose touch (with) 
1.**(与…) 失去联系：**  

 > I regret to say that I have lost touch with four or five friends.   
 > 很遗憾，我已与四五个朋友失去了联系。  （外研⑥ – 3）  
<audio src="./media/touch-17.aac" controls="controls"></audio>
