### 1.*高义频：* **问；询问：**  

 > She asked Mary what time it was.   
 > 她问玛丽几点了。    
<audio src="./media/1-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > We’ll have to ask someone the way to the station.   
 > 我们得去问一下车站怎么走。    
<audio src="./media/2-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > She **asked about** Jesse’s health.   
 > 她询问了杰西的健康状况。    
<audio src="./media/3-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **要求；请求：**  

- ask (sb) to do sth:

 > One evening, a passer-by asked to stay for the night.  
 > 一天晚上，有个过路人请求借宿一晚。  （2008 陕西）  
<audio src="./media/4-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > People may feel forced when asked to do things that they don’t want to.  
 > 当被要求做自己不愿做的事情时，人们会有种被迫的感觉。  （2016 江苏书面表达）  
<audio src="./media/ask50.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- ask + that / wh- 从句:

 > She asked that she should be kept informed of developments.   
 > 她要求向她报告进展情况。    
<audio src="./media/5-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- ask (sb) for sth:

 > Don’t be afraid of asking (others) for help when it is needed.  
 > 需要时不要害怕(向他人)求助。    
<audio src="./media/7-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I went into a cafe and asked for a coffee.   
 > 我走进一家咖啡馆要了杯咖啡。    
<audio src="./media/8-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **邀请：**  

- ask sb to do sth:

 > She’s asked him to come to the party.   
 > 她已邀请他来参加聚会。    
<audio src="./media/9-ask.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*高义频：* **索要 (价格)：**  

 > He’s **asking** ￡80 a month rent **for** that flat.   
 > 那套公寓月租他要价80英镑。

真题解析
---
1. No matter how many times I asked to watching cartoons, my parents would not to let me. (改错)  （2018 全国）  
2. I wasn’t able to hide my eagerness when I _______ (ask), “What do you wish me to do now?”  （2015 湖南改）  
3. Before building a house, you will have to ask _______ the government’s permission.  

答案：
1. watching → watch; 去掉let前的to  2. asked  3. for
