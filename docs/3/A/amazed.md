英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **吃惊的；惊奇的(astonished)：**  

- (be) amazed to do sth:

 > Mr Grant was amazed to ﬁnd 46 ancient gold coins inside the pot.  
 > 格兰特先生惊奇地发现罐子里有46枚古金币。    
<audio src="./media/amazed-101_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) amazed at / by:

 > I’m amazed at / by the progress you’ve made.  
 > 我对你取得的进步感到惊讶。  （2009 湖北改）  
<audio src="./media/I'm amazed_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) amazed + (that) / how-从句：

 > I was amazed that he knew a lot about stamps.  
 > 我很惊讶他对邮票懂得很多。    
<audio src="./media/I was amazed that he knew_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. I was amazing at the fact that a sick person could feel much more better after seeing a doctor.  （2019 全国 改错）  
2. Little Tom sat_______(amaze) watching the monkey dancing in front of him.  （2015 四川改）  

答案：
1. amazing→amazed: 去掉more  2. amazed
