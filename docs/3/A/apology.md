英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **道歉；歉意：**  

 > **accept sb’s apology**  
 > 接受某人的道歉    

 > **owe sb an apology**  
 > 应该向某人道歉    

 > I **demand** an immediate **apology** from you.  
 > 我要求你立即道歉。    
<audio src="./media/1-apology.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I must **make an apology to** him **for** not going to his party.  
 > 没有参加他的聚会，我得向他道歉。    
<audio src="./media/2-apology.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. — Go and say sorry to your Mom, Dave.
— I’d like to, but I’m afraid she won’t be happy with my  ________.  （2015 江苏）  
A.requests  
B.excuses  
C.apologies  
D.regrets  
2. I have an ________ (道歉) to make to you — I’m afraid I opened your letter by mistake.   （2011 陕西）  

答案：
1. C  2. apology
