英文释义

### 1.*高义频：* **恼怒的；烦恼的：**  

- (be) annoyed at / with sb:

 > She was annoyed with Duncan for forgetting to phone.  
 > 邓肯忘了打电话让她很生气。    
<audio src="./media/1-annoyed.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) annoyed about / by sth:

 > Are you annoyed by the quality of your sleep?  
 > 你在为睡眠质量不高而烦恼吗？  （2008 湖南）  
<audio src="./media/2-annoyed.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) annoyed + that 从句：

 > Mr Davies was annoyed that the books were missing.  
 > 那些图书不见了，戴维斯先生很生气。
