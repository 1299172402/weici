 > 两方达成一致比三方达成一致要容易。

### 2.*高义频：* **协定；协议：**  

 > sign an agreement   
 > 签订协议    

 > **arrive at** / **come to** / **reach an agreement** ( **with**)  
 > (与…)达成协议    

 > hold to / keep an agreement  
 > 遵守协议    

 > They had **an agreement** never to talk about work at home** to**  
 > 他们约定在家中绝不谈工作。
