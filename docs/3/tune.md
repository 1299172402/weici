## \#in tune 
1.**合调；协调：**  

 > sing in tune   
 > 唱得合调    

 > be in tune with the times   
 > 跟上时代潮流    

## \#out of tune 
1.**走调；不协调：**  

 > I have no ear for music. My singing is out of tune.  
 > 我不懂音乐，唱歌总跑调。
