真题解析
---
1. Cecily, I shall ________ (想念) our pleasant talks when you leave.  （2015 陕西）  
2. We ________ (未赶上) the last bus and didn’t have any money for taxi, so we had to walk home.  

答案：
1. miss  2. missed
