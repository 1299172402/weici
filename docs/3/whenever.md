真题解析
---
1. _______ I have to give a speech, I get extremely nervous before I start.  （2013 山东）  
A.Whatever  
B.Whenever  
C.Whoever  
D.However  
2. The map is one of the best tools a man has ________ he goes to a new place.  （2012 上海）  
A.whenever  
B.whatever  
C.wherever  
D.however  

答案：
1. B  2. A
