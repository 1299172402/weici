 > a **miracle cure / drug**  
 > 有奇效的疗法 / 特效药    

 > **perform / work miracles**  
 > 创造奇迹；有奇效    

 > **It's a miracle that** these buildings came through the war undamaged.  
 > 这些建筑物历经战争却完好无损，真是个奇迹。
