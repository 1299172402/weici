英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **虽然 (although)：**  

 > Though I need money for myself, I’m still willing to help.   
 > 虽然我自己也需要钱，但我还是乐意帮忙。  （外研③ – 2）  
<audio src="./media/though-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: though意为“虽然”，引导状语从句时，可将从句中的表语、状语等提前，其他成分保持陈述语序。  
 > Much though I admire her, I cannot excuse her faults.   
 > 虽然我非常崇拜她，但我不能原谅她的错误。    
<audio src="./media/though-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*低义频：* **即使：**  

 > Though I fail, I shall try again.   
 > 即使失败，我也要再试一次。    
<audio src="./media/though-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **可是；不过；然而：**  

 > He’ll probably say no, though it’s worth asking.   
 > 他很可能会拒绝，不过问一下也没什么坏处。    
<audio src="./media/though-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. Though not very big, but the restaurant is popular in our area.  （2016 全国 改错）  
2. If scientists have learned a lot about the universe, there is much we still don’t know. 
  （2015安徽改 改错）  
3. Unsatisfied _________ with the payment, he took the job just to get some work experience.  （2009 重庆）  
A.though was he  
B.though he was  
C.he was though  
D.was he though  

答案：
1. 去掉but  2. If → Though / Although  3. B
