英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **vt & vi (使) 弯曲；(使) 扭曲：**  

 > Twist the wire to form a circle.   
 > 把金属丝弯成一个环。    
<audio src="./media/twist-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > His face twisted with pain.   
 > 他痛苦得脸都变形了。    
<audio src="./media/twist-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **vt & vi 转动 (身体等)：**  

 > The visitor twisted his neck to look at the photograph on the wall behind him.   
 > 参观者扭过头看他身后墙上的照片。    
<audio src="./media/twist-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > He twisted the cap off the bottle.   
 > 他旋开了瓶盖。    
<audio src="./media/twist-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **vt 扭伤：**  

 > Having **twisted her ankle**, our tour guide had to be taken to the hospital.   
 > 我们的导游扭伤了脚踝，不得不被送到医院。  （译林⑧ – 3）  
<audio src="./media/twist-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*高义频：* **vi 盘绕；盘旋；蜿蜒：**  

 > A snake was **twisting around** his arm.   
 > 一条蛇盘绕在他的手臂上。    
<audio src="./media/twist-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The river **twists and turns** through the green fields.  
 > 河流蜿蜒穿过绿色的田野。    
<audio src="./media/twist-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 5.*低义频：* **vt 歪曲；曲解：**  

 > The papers twisted everything I said.   
 > 报纸把我说的一切都歪曲了。    
<audio src="./media/twist-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>
