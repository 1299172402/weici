英文释义

### 1.*高义频：* **高兴的；快乐的**  
英译 very pleased and happy：

 > a delighted look   
 > 高兴的神情    

- (be) delighted at / by / with:

 > I was delighted at the news of her wedding.  
 > 我很高兴听到她婚礼的消息。    
<audio src="./media/delighted-101_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) delighted to do sth:

 > The professor was delighted to find that the project had been finished by the students independently.  
 > 教授高兴地发现学生们已独立完成了该项目。  （2015 湖北）  
<audio src="./media/P125 delighted-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

-  (be) delighted + (that) 从句：

 > We’re delighted (that) you’re coming to work with us.  
 > 我们很高兴你要来和我们一起工作。  （人教⑤ – 4）  
<audio src="./media/delighted-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: 不能用very修饰delighted。
