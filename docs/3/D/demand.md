### 1.*高义频：* **[C] 要求：**  

 > a **demand for** higher wages   
 > 加薪的要求    

 > **meet** / **satisfy** the **demands** of the customers (= to give them what they are asking for)  
 > 满足客户要求    

 > **Due to popular demand**, a writing project will be started for eager beginners.   
 > 应公众的要求，一个为急切的初学者设立的写作项目即将开展。  （2014 湖南）  
<audio src="./media/P126 demand n.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **[C] & [U] 需要；需求：**  

 > **supply and demand**   
 > 供求    

 > In America the **demand for** oil has grown by 22 percent since 1990.  
 > 1990年以来，美国对石油的需求增长了22%。  （2018 浙江）

英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **(坚决) 要求：**  

 > Taylor was able to demand very high pay for her movies.    
 > 泰勒能要求很高的片酬。    
<audio src="./media/demand-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- demand to do sth:

 > She demanded to see the manager.   
 > 她坚决要求见经理。    
<audio src="./media/demand-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- demand + that ... (should) do sth:

 > Her teachers demanded that she wear longer skirts.  
 > 老师要求她穿长些的裙子。  （ 外研⑦ – 6）  
<audio src="./media/demand-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: 不能用demand sb to do sth。  
### 2.*高义频：* **需要：**  

 > This work demands care and patience.   
 > 这项工作需要细心和耐心。
