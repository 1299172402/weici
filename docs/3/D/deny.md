英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **否认；否定：**  

 > He denied all charges.   
 > 他否认所有指控。    
<audio src="./media/deny-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > **There is no denying that** protecting the environment is of great importance.   
 > 不可否认保护环境十分重要。    
<audio src="./media/deny-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- deny doing sth：

 > He denied taking money but we were sceptical.   
 > 他否认收受了贿赂，但我们很怀疑。  （人教⑤ – 4）  
<audio src="./media/deny-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- deny + that 从句：

 > I never deny that you're a good actor.  
 > 我从不否认你是位好演员。    
<audio src="./media/I never deny that_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **拒绝承认；拒绝接受；拒绝给予：**  

- deny sb sth / deny sth to sb:

 > No one should be denied a good education. / A good education should be denied to no one.  
 > 任何人都不能被剥夺接受良好教育的权利。    
<audio src="./media/No one should be denied_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. — Are you still mad at her?
— Not really, but I can’t ________ (否认) that her remarks hurt me.   （2011 江苏）  
A.deny  
B.refuse  
C.reject  
D.decline  

答案：
1. deny
