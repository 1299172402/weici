英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **喝；喝 (酒)：**  

 > **drink** hard / **heavily**   
 > 酗酒    

 > She wants to drink juice.   
 > 她要喝果汁。    
<audio src="./media/drink-1 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > They drank themselves stupid.   
 > 他们喝得晕头转向。    
<audio src="./media/drink-2 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Don't **drink and drive**.  
 > 切勿酒后驾车。    
<audio src="./media/drink-101_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: “喝水”要说drink water；用汤匙“喝汤”要 说eat soup，用杯子“喝汤”用drink soup；“吃药”要说take medicine。  

固定搭配
---
## \#drink sb’s health
1.**为某人的健康干杯**  

## \#drink to sb / sth
1.**为某人 / 某事干杯：**  

 > Let's drink to your success in your new job.  
 > 让我们为你在新工作中取得成功而干杯。
