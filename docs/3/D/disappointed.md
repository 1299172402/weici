### 1.*高义频：* **失望的；沮丧的：**  

 > One by one, the applicants left the interviewer’s office with disappointed looks on their faces.  
 > 一个接着一个，求职者面带失望的表情离开了面试间。  （2013 山东）  
<audio src="./media/disappointed-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) disappointed at / about / with：

 > The teacher soon realized why the mother was so disappointed about the grade.   
 > 老师很快就明白了为什么母亲对这个成绩如此失望。  （2012 上海改）  
<audio src="./media/disappointed-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > She left the restaurant, disappointed with the bad service.   
 > 对餐馆糟糕的服务非常不满，她离开了。  （译林⑤ – 3）  
<audio src="./media/disappointed-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) disappointed with / in sb:

 > I’m disappointed in you — I really thought I could trust you!  
 > 你让我很失望，我原以为可以信任你的！    
<audio src="./media/I'm disappointed_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) disappointed to do sth:

 > We were **deeply / terribly disappointed** to find the museum was closed.  
 > 发现博物馆关闭了，我们非常失望。  （外研⑧ – 7）  
<audio src="./media/We were deeply.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) disappointed + (that) 从句：

 > My mother said it was a great day, but she was just a little disappointed that it didn’t last a tiny bit longer.  
 > 我母亲认为那天很不错，但她还是有点失望，要是持续时间稍微再长点就好了。  （2009 湖北）
