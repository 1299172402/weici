固定搭配

## \#cut back
1.**缩减；削减**  
英译  to reduce the amount, size, cost etc. of sth：

 > Several companies are **cutting back on** staff at the moment.   
 > 目前，有几家公司正在裁员。

## \#cut down
1.*高义频：* **砍倒：**

 > 人们伐树掘草，也会造成土壤沙化。  （外研③ – 4）  
<audio src="./media/cut-15.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

2.*高义频：* **减少；缩减**

 > If you want to lose weight you have to **cut down on** fatty foods.

## \#cut in

## \#cut off

<audio src="./media/cut-22.aac" controls="controls"></audio>
