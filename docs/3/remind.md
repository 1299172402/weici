英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **提醒**  
英译 to make sb remember sth that they must do：

 > **Remind you**, Jane. Take care. My wife got her purse picked at the station once.   
 > 提醒你一下，简，要当心。我妻子的钱包曾在火车站被偷过。    
<audio src="./media/remind-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- remind sb about / of:

 > I forgot to remind him about / of the time for the interview.   
 > 我忘了提醒他面试的时间。    
<audio src="./media/remind-3-.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- remind sb to do sth:

 > We put up a board to remind people to protect the trees.  
 > 我们竖起一块牌子提醒人们保护树木。  （2009 北京）  
<audio src="./media/remind-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- remind sb + (that) / wh-从句：

 > She reminded me that I hadn’t called Mother.  
 > 她提醒我还没有给妈妈打电话。  （2011 全国改）  
<audio src="./media/She reminded me that_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Do something you’re good at. Remind yourself how good you are at so many things.   
 > 做点你擅长的事，要提醒自己你是多么的多才多艺。    
<audio src="./media/remind-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **使想起**  
英译 to make sb remember sb that they know or sth that happened in the past：

- remind sb of sb / sth:

 > Bill reminded him of the day years ago when they had first met.   
 > 比尔使他想起多年前他们初识的那一天。    
<audio src="./media/remind-8-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — Brad was Jane’s brother!   
 > 布拉德是简的兄弟。    
<audio src="./media/remind-9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — No wonder he reminded me so much of Jane!  
 > 难怪他让我觉得他那么像简呢。

真题解析
---
1. The photo will remind me ________ the days when we were together.  （2007 浙江改）  
2. In our childhood, we were often ________ (提醒) by Grandma to pay attention to our table manners.  （2005 江苏改）  

答案：
1. of  2. reminded
