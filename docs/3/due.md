英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **预定的；预期的**  
英译 arranged or expected to happen or arrive at a particular time：

 > Please make sure you return the book before it’s due.  
 > 请确保在到期前还书。  （2012 全国）  
<audio src="./media/due57.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > After only six months he was **due for** a raise if this assessment was satisfactory.  
 > 如果这份评价令人满意，仅6个月后他就会得到加薪。    
<audio src="./media/due51.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- due to do sth:

 > She is due to complete her study and graduate later this year.  
 > 她今年下半年就要完成学业毕业了。  （2008 天津）  
<audio src="./media/due52.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **(钱) 到期的：**  

 > The next income tax payment is due on January 31.  
 > 下一次的所得税支付期限是1月31日。    
<audio src="./media/due53.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*低义频：* **应得的；欠下的**  
英译 owed to sb either as a debt or because they have done sth to deserve it：

 > Our thanks are **due to** the whole team.  
 > 我们要向全队致谢。    
<audio src="./media/due54.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The wages **due to** him will be paid tomorrow.  
 > 他应得的工资明天付给他。    
<audio src="./media/due55.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*低义频：* **适当的；合适的**  
英译 proper or suitable：

 > No matter how ordinary a job is, it plays a part in society and therefore deserves our due respect.  
 > 无论工作多么普通，都在社会中起着作用，因此值得我们尊敬。  （2013 广东书面表达）  
<audio src="./media/due56.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#due to
1.**由于；因为：**  

 > The town was cut off from the rest of the world due to heavy snow.  
 > 由于暴雪，这个小镇与外界隔绝了。  （2016 北京）  
<audio src="./media/due58.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The water shortage is severe mainly due to global warming, environmental pollution and the everincreasing population.  
 > 水资源严重短缺主要是由全球变暖、环境污染和人口持续增长造成的。  （2010 福建书面表达改）
