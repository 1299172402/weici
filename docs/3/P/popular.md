### 1.*高义频：* **流行的；受欢迎的**  
反义词 unpopular： 

 > Online voting becomes **increasingly popular**, and many competitions get people involved in it.  
 > 在线投票越来越盛行，很多比赛都让人们参与进来投票。  （2016 江苏书面表达）

## \#be popular with / among
1.**为…喜欢；受…欢迎：**  

 > The mobile phone is fashionable and convenient to carry, so it is very popular with youngsters.  
 > 这款手机样式新颖、携带方便，深受年轻人的欢迎。    
<audio src="./media/popular-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Smart and pretty, Kate is very popular among her friends.  
 > 凯特既聪明又漂亮，深受朋友们的喜爱。    
<audio src="./media/popular-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **公众的；大众化的；通俗的：**  

 > **a popular belief / opinion**  
 > 普遍的观念/民意    

 > What we need most are popular science books.  
 > 我们最需要的是科普类图书。  （2014 浙江书面表达）  
<audio src="./media/popular-What we need most are popular science books.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > 用通俗的语言
