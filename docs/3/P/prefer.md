英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **更喜欢；更愿意：**  

 > — Tea or coffee?  
 > 喝茶还是咖啡？    
<audio src="./media/prefer--1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — I’d prefer tea, thanks.   
 > 我更喜欢茶，谢谢。    

 > She prefers her coffee black.   
 > 她更喜欢喝清咖啡。    
<audio src="./media/prefer-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prefer sb / sth to do sth:

 > I’d prefer you to start early.   
 > 我更希望你早点出发。  （北师大⑧ – 24）  
<audio src="./media/prefer03.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I prefer my English classes to be taught in English only.   
 > 我希望我的英语课是全英语授课。    
<audio src="./media/prefer-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prefer to do sth / doing sth:

 > Some people prefer to do almost everything over the Internet.   
 > 有些人喜欢在网上做几乎一切事情。  （ 2013 安徽）  
<audio src="./media/prefer-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I prefer shutting myself in and listening to music all day on Sunday.   
 > 周日我喜欢一整天把自己关在屋里听音乐。    
<audio src="./media/prefer-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prefer (doing) sth to (doing) sth:

 > I prefer the red dress to the green one because it fits me better.   
 > 比起那条绿裙子，我更喜欢那条红色的，因为它更适合我。  （人教① – 3）  
<audio src="./media/prefer-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I would prefer reading books to watching television.  
 > 比起看电视，我还是喜欢读书。    
<audio src="./media/prefer-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prefer + that 从句：

 > They preferred the money (should) be used for building schools and hospitals.   
 > 他们更愿意拿这笔钱来建学校和医院。    
<audio src="./media/prefer-9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I would prefer that you didn’t mention my name.   
 > 我希望你不要提到我的名字。    
<audio src="./media/prefer-10.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: prefer + that 从句结构中，从句中用虚拟语气。  

派生词汇
---
preferred  /prɪ'fəd/ adj   

真题解析
---

答案：
1. prefer
