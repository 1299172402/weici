英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **骄傲的；自豪的**  
反义词 ashamed ： 

 > a proud look  
 > 得意的表情    

- (be) proud of sb / sth:

 > We are very proud of ourselves and believe we can do more for a better world.  
 > 我们非常自豪，并相信为了让世界更美好，我们可以做得更多。  （2016 北京书面表达）  
<audio src="./media/proud50.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) proud to do sth:

 > She is proud to have taken part in competitions and to have broken a record.  
 > 她对自己参加比赛并打破了纪录感到自豪。  （人教⑦ – 1）  
<audio src="./media/proud-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) proud + that 从句：

 > She was proud that her daughter had so much talent.  
 > 女儿这么有天赋，她很自豪。    
<audio src="./media/proud-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **[含贬义] 妄自尊大的；自负的：**  

 > She is too proud to see her own mistake.  
 > 她太自负了，看不到自己的错误。
