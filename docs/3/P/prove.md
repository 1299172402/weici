英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **证明；证实：**  

 > Riis believed that poverty caused crime, and he used photos to help him **prove his point**.  
 > 理斯认为贫穷导致犯罪，他还借助照片来证明自己的观点。  （2008 陕西）  
<audio src="./media/prove-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prove sth to sb:

 > Just give me a chance and I’ll prove it to you.  
 > 给我一个机会，我会证明给你看。    
<audio src="./media/prove-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: 表示向某人证明某事时应该用prove sth to sb，而不能用prove sb sth。  
- prove sb / sth (to be) + adj / n:

 > They put me in prison, but, in the end, I was proved innocent.  
 > 他们把我关进监狱，但我最终被证明是清白的。  （北师大③ – 3）  
<audio src="./media/prove-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > He proved himself (to be) a better driver than the world champion.  
 > 他证明了自己的驾驶技术胜过世界冠军。    
<audio src="./media/prove-He proved himself to.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prove + that / wh-从句：

 > Sarah wants to prove that she has brains as well as beauty.  
 > 萨拉想证明她既有美貌，又有头脑。  （译林⑦）  
<audio src="./media/Sarah wants to prove that she_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The challenge proved how difficult it is to drive across a desert without guidance.  
 > 这个挑战证明在没有引导的情况下开车穿越沙漠是多么的困难。    
<audio src="./media/prove-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- it is proved that:

 > It has been proved that eating vegetables in childhood helps to protect you against serious illnesses in later life.  
 > 经证实，童年时吃蔬菜有助于日后不生大病。  （人教② – 1）  
<audio src="./media/prove-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prove oneself (to sb):

 > When I first started this job, I felt I had to prove myself.  
 > 我刚开始干这份工作的时候，觉得必须证明一下自己的实力。    
<audio src="./media/prove-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **[用作系动词] 证明是；被发现是 (turn out)：**  

- prove+adj/n:

 > The book has proved very successful.  
 > 事实证明了这本书非常成功。  （人教⑧ – 3）

 > 事实表明他是一个诚实的朋友。
