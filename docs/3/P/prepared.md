### 1.*高义频：* **准备好的；有所准备的：**  
反义词 unprepared 

 > Our monitor presented our teacher with the prepared flowers.  
 > 我们班长给老师献上了准备好的花。  （2012 陕西书面表达）  
<audio src="./media/prepared-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > It seems you’re **well prepared**.   
 > 看来你做好了充分的准备。  （2008 重庆）  
<audio src="./media/prepared-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- be / get prepared for:

 > We should improve our study methods and get well prepared for examinations.   
 > 我们应该改进学习方法，为考试做好充分准备。    
<audio src="./media/prepared-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- be / get prepared to do sth:

 > All the competitors must **be fully prepared to** give the best performance.  
 > 所有参赛者一定要做好充分准备，以便发挥最佳水平。  （2009 天津书面表达改）
