### 1.*高义频：* **[不用于名词前]高兴的；满意的：**  

- (be) pleased to do sth:

 > Her parents were so pleased to get the news that she won the prize.   
 > 得知她获奖的消息，她的父母非常高兴。  （人教③ – 5）  
<audio src="./media/pleased-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) pleased + (that) 从句：

 > Everyone is pleased that you’ve decided to come to the party.   
 > 你决定来参加聚会大家都十分高兴。    
<audio src="./media/pleased-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- (be) pleased with:

 > I am very pleased with the quality of your work.   
 > 我对你的工作质量很满意。
