英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **预言；预计 (forecast)：**  

 > predict tomorrow’s weather  
 > 预告明天的天气    

- predict + (that) / wh- 从句：

 > Population experts predict that most people will live in cities in the near future.   
 > 人口专家预言，在不久的将来大多数人都会住到城市里。  （2008 上海春）  
<audio src="./media/predict-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > It’s hard to predict when driverless cars will be everywhere on our roads.  
 > 很难预料何时我们的公路上才会出现大量的无人驾驶汽车。  （2018 北京）  
<audio src="./media/It’s hard to predict when_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- it is predicted + that 从句：

 > It was predicted that inflation would continue to fall.  
 > 据预测，通货膨胀率将继续下降。
