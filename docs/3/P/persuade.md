英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **使相信；使信服 (convince)：**  

- persuade sb of sth:

 > I’m persuaded of his honesty.   
 > 我相信他是诚实的。    
<audio src="./media/persuade-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- persuade sb + that 从句:

 > I persuaded him that I was telling the truth.   
 > 我使他相信我讲的是实情。    
<audio src="./media/persuade-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **说服；劝服 (talk into)：**  

 > She is always easily persuaded.   
 > 她总是很容易被说服。    
<audio src="./media/persuade-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- persuade sb to do sth:

 > She persuaded me to go to a dental school.  
 > 她说服了我去牙科学校学习。    
<audio src="./media/She persuaded me to_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- persuade sb into (doing) sth:

 > I managed to persuade my classmates into accepting my idea.  
 > 我设法说服了我的同学接受我的想法。  （2015 浙江书面表达）  
<audio src="./media/P323 persuade1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- persuade sb out of (doing) sth:

 > We persuaded him out of his foolish plan.   
 > 我们说服了他放弃那项愚蠢的计划。    
<audio src="./media/persuade-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: 在表示“劝说”，但不一定“劝服”时，用try to persuade。  
 > She would try to persuade local business leaders to contribute money to the cause.  
 > 她会劝说当地的商界领导人为该事业捐款。  （2017 北京改）
