---

2. Marty ________ (work) really hard on his book and he thinks he’ll have finished it by Friday.  （2015 陕西改）  
A.worked  
B.has been working  
C.had worked  
D.has worked  
3. You can’t predict everything. Often things don’t work ________as you expect.  （2011 江西改）  

答案：
1. working  2. B  3. out
