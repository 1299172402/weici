## \#in the air 
1.**(某种情绪) 弥漫在空气中；即将发生：**  

 > There was a sense of excitement in the air.   
 > 到处洋溢着一种激动的气氛。    
<audio src="./media/5-air.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Change is in the air.   
 > 变革迫在眉睫。
