英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **[作how的强调形式] 究竟怎样；到底如何：**  

 > However did you know that?  
 > 你到底是怎样知道那件事的？    
<audio src="./media/however-1 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > However did you make such a mistake?  
 > 你怎么会犯这样的错？    
<audio src="./media/however-2 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **[相当于no matter how，引导让步状语从句] 无论如何；不管怎样：**  

 > However hard you shout, Granny won't be able to hear you.  
 > 不管你叫得多响，奶奶都听不到你的声音。  （外研⑧ – 7）  
<audio src="./media/however-3 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > He tried his best to solve the problem, however difficult it was.  
 > 不管问题有多难，他都会尽力解决。    
<audio src="./media/however-4 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **[句子副词] 但是；不过；然而：**  

 > The composition is all right; there's room for improvement, however.  
 > 这篇作文不错，不过还有改进的余地。    
<audio src="./media/however-5 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Sailing in rough weather can be very unpleasant. However, we found it exciting.  
 > 天气不好的时候航行很可能不畅快。然而，我们却觉得很刺激。    
<audio src="./media/however-6 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > His first response was to say no. Later, however, he changed his mind.  
 > 他最初的反应是不同意，但是，他后来改变了主意。    
<audio src="./media/however-7 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: however作此用法时，可置于句首、句中或句末，但必须与句子的其他成分用逗号分隔开来。  

真题解析
---
1. Queen ElizabethⅡ is often thought to be the richest woman in the world. _______, her personal wealth seems rather small.  （2012 安徽改）  
2. ________ hard you try, it is difficult to lose weight without cutting down the amount you eat.  （2012 湖南改）  

答案：
1. However  2. However / No matter how
