英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **欠 (债)：**  

- owe sb sth (for sth) / owe sth to sb (for sth):

 > She still owes her father $5,000. / She still owes $5,000 to her father.  
 > 她还欠她父亲5000美元。    
<audio src="./media/owe-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **应给予：**  

- owe sb sth / owe sth to sb:

 > I owe him an apology. / I owe an apology to him.  
 > 我得向他道歉。    
<audio src="./media/owe-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*低义频：* **应该把...归功于：**  

- owe sth to sth:

 > He owes his good health to his regular life.  
 > 他把自身的健康归功于生活有规律。    
<audio src="./media/owe-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > These early settlers owed their survival to hard work and determination to succeed.   
 > 这些早期的移民把他们的幸存归功于艰苦的劳动和必胜的决心。  （人教⑨ – 3）
