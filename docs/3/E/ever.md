英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **在 (以往) 任何时候；从来；曾经：**  

 > Nothing new ever happens in this little town.   
 > 这个小镇从不发生新鲜事。    
<audio src="./media/ever-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Did you ever visit her those days?   
 > 那段时间你去看望过她吗？    
<audio src="./media/ever-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > If you ever come to London, please let me know.   
 > 你若来伦敦，请告诉我。    
<audio src="./media/ever-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > It's raining **harder than ever**.  
 > 雨下得更大了。    
<audio src="./media/ever-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > He is one of the best men I have ever met.   
 > 他是我遇到过的最好的人之一    
<audio src="./media/ever-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: ever常用于否定句、一般疑问句以及表示条件和比较的从句，也可与比较级或最高级连用。  
### 2.*低义频：* **究竟；到底：**  

 > Why ever did you do so?   
 > 你究竟为什么要这么做？    
<audio src="./media/ever-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


固定搭配
---
## \#ever after 
1.**从那时以后一直**  

## \#ever since 
1.**自从；从…以来；此后一直：**  

 > Their work has influenced other writers ever since.   
 > 他们的作品此后一直影响着后世的作家。  （外研③ – 1）  
<audio src="./media/ever-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The Project Hope has helped numerous poor students ever since it was founded.  
 > 希望工程自创建以来已经帮助了无数的贫困学生。

真题解析
---
1. Attention, coffee lovers! We have for you, the best coffee machine ________invented.  （2006 江西）  
A.ever  
B.already  
C.even  
D.nowadays  
2. I thought she was famous, but none of my friends have ________heard of her.  （2006 广东）  
A.even  
B.ever  
C.just  
D.never  

答案：
1. A  2. B
