### 1.**依靠；依赖；指望**  
英译  to depend on sb or sth, especially in a difficult situation：

 > That was the first time Dad had counted on me in a moment of emergency.  
 > 这是父亲第一次在危急时刻依赖我。  （2011 重庆）
