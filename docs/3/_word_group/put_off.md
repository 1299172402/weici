### 1.*高义频：* **延期；推迟 (delay / postpone)：**  

 > She called this afternoon to put off the meeting to next week.   
 > 她下午打电话说把会议推迟到下周。    
<audio src="./media/put-33.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **使反感；使不信任：**  

 > She is very clever but her manner does tend to put people off.   
 > 她很聪明，但态度令人反感。    
<audio src="./media/put-34.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **打扰；使分心：**  

 > Don’t put me off when I’m trying to concentrate.   
 > 在我想要集中精力的时候别打扰我。    
<audio src="./media/put-35.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*高义频：* **使对...失去兴趣：**  

 > Don’t let your failures put you off trying harder.  
 > 别因为失败你就不再进一步努力了。
