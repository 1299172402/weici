# ***too*** adv  重难点词汇
英音 tuː     美音 tuː

英文释义
---

> I have a stomachache, and a headache, too.   
> 我肚子疼，头也疼。

> — I’m hungry.   
> 我饿了。

> — Me, too.   
> 我也是。

2. *高义频：* **太；过分：**  


- #### too + adj + a / an + n …:

> It was too difficult a book for her.   
> 那本书对她来说太难了。

- #### too + adj / adv + (for sb / sth) to do sth:

> The problem is too complicated for us to solve.   
> 那个问题太复杂，我们无法解决。

> The coat is too large for me.  
> 这件外套我穿太大了。


固定搭配
---
- #### all too 
1. **太：**  


> Accidents like this happen all too often.   
> 这类事故发生得太频繁了。

- #### can’t / can never ... too ... 
1. **怎么…也不过分； 越…越好：**  


> — Must I turn off the gas after cooking?    
> 做完饭后一定要把煤气关掉吗？

> — Of course. You can never be too careful with that.  
> 当然，你越小心越好。

### 辨析 much too, too much


真题解析
---

— Try not to work yourself too hard. Take it easy.
— Thanks.________ (你也是).  
##### 答案 You, too

### 辨析 manage, try

> **Try,luck**  
> Try your strength / luck.   
> 试试你的力气/碰碰你的运气吧。

> If I’m going out for a meal, I prefer to try something different.   
> 如果到外面吃饭，我喜欢尝试不同的饭菜。

1. try doing sth 一般表示“尝试做某事”，含 有试试看的意味；try to do sth 则表示“努力做某 事”。2. 口语中，尤其在祈使句中，常用try and 代替 try to。
