英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **（天气）转晴；（烟雾等）消散；（液体）变清澈：**  

 > The sky cleared after the storm.  
 > 暴风雨过后，天转晴了。    
<audio src="./media/6-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The fog usually clears by midday.  
 > 雾一般在中午前散去。    
<audio src="./media/The fog usually clear_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **清理；清除；疏散：**  

 > clear the table  
 > 收拾桌子    

 > After three years, Dennis cleared several fields and planted crops.  
 > 三年后，丹尼斯开垦了几块田地并种上了庄稼。  （2013 江西改）  
<audio src="./media/After three years 2_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > After the bomb warning, police cleared the streets.  
 > 接到有炸弹的警告后，警察疏散了街上的行人。    
<audio src="./media/9-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **恢复畅通：**  

 > The traffic took a long time to clear after the accident.  
 > 事故发生后很长时间交通才恢复畅通。    
<audio src="./media/10-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*低义频：* **(使) 变清晰；(使) 变清醒：**  

 > clear one’s head  
 > 使头脑清醒    

 > As my mind cleared, I remembered what had happened.  
 > 头脑清醒后，我想起了所发生的一切。    
<audio src="./media/11-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#clear away
1.**把…清除掉：**  

 > clear away the bush near houses  
 > 清除房子附近的灌木    

## \#clear off
1.**离开；逃跑：**  

 > He suddenly cleared off when a nurse asked who he was.  
 > 当护士询问他是谁时，他突然离开了。    
<audio src="./media/12-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

1.**清清嗓子**  

## \#clear out
1.**把…清空：**  

 > clear the room out  
 > 把房间腾空    

## \#clear the air
1.**缓解紧张状态：**  

 > Our conversation was filled with arguments; and efforts to clear the air seemed to deepen our misunderstanding.  
 > 我们的谈话充满了争论；为缓解紧张气氛所作出的努力似乎都在加深误会。  （2010 全国改）  
<audio src="./media/13-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#clear up
1.**(天气) 转晴；清理；解决：**  

 > clear the room up  
 > 清理房间    

 > clear up a mystery / difficulty / misunderstanding  
 > 揭开谜团/解决困难/消除误会

英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **清澈的；透明的(transparent)；晴朗的：**  

 > the clear water of a lake  
 > 清澈的湖水    

 > clear glass  
 > 透明的玻璃    

 > a clear sky / day  
 > 晴空/晴天    

### 2.*高义频：* **清楚的；明显的；明确的(plain)：**  
反义词 unclear 

 > a clear photograph / voice  
 > 清晰的照片/声音    

 > have a clear advantage in the contest  
 > 在比赛中有明显的优势    

 > have a clear purpose / goal  
 > 有明确的目标  （2009 江苏）  

 > Ladies and gentlemen, have I **made myself clear**?  
 > 女士们、先生们，我讲清楚了吗？  （2009 湖北书面表达改）  
<audio src="./media/1-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- be clear about / on:

 > If you’re clear about where you want to go, the rest of the world will either help you get there or get out of the way.   
 > 如果你清楚自己想去哪儿，世界上其余的一切都会帮你到达那里或为你让路。  （2014 全国）  
<audio src="./media/P86 clear adj-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- it is clear + (that) / wh- 从句：

 > It is clear that your life in your country is quite different from mine.  
 > 很显然，你们国家的生活方式与我的大不相同。    
<audio src="./media/4-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > It was not clear why the man hadn’t reported the accident sooner.  
 > 人们尚不清楚那个人为什么没有早一点报告这起事故。  （2011 江苏）  
<audio src="./media/5-clear.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **无障碍的：**  

 > a clear view / road   
 > 一览无余/畅通的道路    

### 4.*高义频：* **(头脑) 清醒的；（思路）清晰的：：**  

 > keep a clear head / mind  
 > 保持清醒的头脑
