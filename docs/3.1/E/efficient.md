### 1.*高义频：* **有效率的；高效能的**  
英译 able to do sth well without wasting time, money or energy
反义词 inefficient 

 > an efficient secretary  
 > 高效率的秘书    

 > fuel-efficient car / energy-efficient light bulbs  
 > 节能汽车/节能灯泡    

 > You must find a more efficient way of organizing your time.  
 > 你必须找到一个能更有效地安排时间的方法。
