固定搭配
---
## \#as soon as
1.*低义频：* **一...就：**  

 > As soon as I hear my alarm clock, I jump out of my bed.  
 > 我一听到闹钟响就会跳下床。  （北师大① – 1）  
<audio src="./media/soon-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

2.*低义频：* **如...那么早 / 快：**  

 > They didn’t arrive as soon as we had expected.  
 > 他们到得没有我们预期的那么早。    
<audio src="./media/soon-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#no sooner ... than
1.**一…就：**  

 > I had no sooner reached home than it began to rain. / No sooner had I reached home than it began to rain.  
 > 我刚到家就开始下雨了。    
<audio src="./media/soon-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: no sooner置于句首时，句子要用部分倒装结构。  
 > No sooner had he sat down than he heard someone knock at the door.  
 > 他刚坐下来就听见有人敲门。    
<audio src="./media/soon-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

## \#sooner or later 
1.**迟早：**  

 > Sooner or later she was going to awake anyhow.  
 > 不管怎样，她迟早会醒悟过来。    
<audio src="./media/soon-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. No sooner ________ stepped on the stage than the audience broke into thunderous applause.  （2014 陕西）  
A.had Mo Yan  
B.Mo Yan had  
C.has Mo Yan  
D.Mo Yan has  
2. He had no sooner finished his speech ________ the students started cheering.  （2011 辽宁改）  
3. Just use this room for the time being, and we’ll offer you a larger one ________ (一…就) it becomes available.  （2010 安徽改）  

答案：
1. A  2. than  3. as soon as
