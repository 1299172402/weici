### 1.*高义频：* **满意；满足；乐事**  
反义词 dissatisfaction ： 

 > a **feeling / sense of satisfaction**   
 > 满足感    

 > She **expressed** her **satisfaction with / at** the result.  
 > 她对结果表示满意。

## \#to sb’s satisfaction 
1.**令某人满意的是：**  

 > To my satisfaction, I have passed the examination.  
 > 我通过了考试，心满意足。

1.**满意地：**  

 > He looked at his students with great satisfaction.   
 > 他十分满意地看着学生们。
