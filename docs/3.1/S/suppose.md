英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **认为；猜想**  
英译 to think that sth is probable：

- suppose (that) …:

 > I suppose (that) price will go up.   
 > 我认为价格会上涨。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — Will it rain tomorrow?   
 > 明天会下雨吗？    
<audio src="./media/suppose-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — I suppose so. / I suppose not.   
 > 我想会/我想不会。    

Notes: 常用so或not来代替that从句进行回答。  
- I don’t suppose (that) …:

 > I don’t suppose anyone will volunteer, will they?   
 > 我觉得没有人会自告奋勇，是不是？    
<audio src="./media/suppose-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: suppose后的that从句的否定形式通常转移到 suppose上。主句主语是I时，反意疑问句仍然跟从句相关，且用肯定形式。  
- When / Why / What / … do you suppose …?

 > When do you suppose we should leave?   
 > 你认为我们应该什么时候动身？    
<audio src="./media/suppose-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: 上例中，when should we leave用陈述句式。  
- suppose ... to be / to do sth:

 > I shall suppose him to be about fifty.   
 > 我想他应该在50岁左右。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > We suppose them to have finished their homework.  
 > 我们想他们已经完成作业了。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- suppose ... + adj / n:

 > She had supposed him (to be) very rich.   
 > 她原以为他很有钱。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > I had supposed his wife a younger woman.   
 > 我原以为他的妻子更年轻。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-8-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **假定；假设**  
英译 to pretend that sth will happen or is true：

- suppose (that) …:

 > Suppose (that) you have one million dollars — what would you do?   
 > 假如你有一百万美金，你会做什么？    
<audio src="./media/suppose-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- suppose ... to be / to do sth:

 > Let’s suppose the old woman to be your mother.   
 > 我们假定那位老妇人是你的母亲。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- suppose ... + adj / n:

 > Suppose the hero dead — what then？  
 > 假设男主角死了，那怎么办？    
<audio src="./media/suppose-10.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **[表示婉转提建议或勉强同意]：**  

 > Suppose we go for a swim.   
 > 咱们去游泳吧。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-11.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — Can I borrow the car?   
 > 我能借车一用吗？    
<audio src="./media/suppose-12.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > — Oh, **I suppose so**.   
 > 噢，可以吧。    


固定搭配
---
## \#(be) supposed to be / to do sth
1.*高义频：* **被认为；被当作：**  

 > I haven’t seen it myself, but it is supposed to be a great movie.   
 > 我没看过这部电影，但是据说很不错。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-13.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

2.*高义频：* **应该；理应：**  

 > The college years are supposed to be a time for important growth in dependence.  
 > 大学期间应是培养独立性的重要时期。  （2016 北京改）  
<audio src="./media/suppose50.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > You’re not supposed to smoke here.   
 > 这里不可以吸烟。    
<audio src="./media/suppose-15.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


真题解析
---
1. What are you doing out of bed, Tom? You’re s_________ to be asleep.   （2011 辽宁改）  
2. The message is very important, so it is supposed __________ (send) as soon as possible.  （2008 陕西改）  
3. — You should apologize to her, Barry.
— I suppose _________, but it’s not going to be easy.   

答案：
1. supposed  2. to be sent   3. so
