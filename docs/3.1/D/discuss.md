### 1.*高义频：* **讨论；商议：**  

- discuss sth with sb:

 > I’ll discuss the problem with him later.   
 > 稍后我将与他讨论这个问题。    
<audio src="./media/discuss-1 .aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- discuss + wh- 从句 / wh- to do：

 > We discussed how to do it and who should do it.  
 > 我们讨论了怎样做这件事和由谁做这件事。
