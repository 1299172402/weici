### 1.*高义频：* **（机器等）出故障；（身体等）垮掉；（情感等）失控：**  

 > On my way to the station my car broke down.   
 > 去车站的路上，我的车坏了。  （人教⑤ – 2）  
<audio src="./media/17-break.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Her health broke down under the pressure of work.  
 > 她的身体在工作压力下垮掉了。    
<audio src="./media/18-break.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > He broke down as he thought of his failure to sell all his newspapers.  
 > 想到没卖掉所有的报纸，他禁不住哭了起来。  （2015 湖北）  
<audio src="./media/Break-103_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>
