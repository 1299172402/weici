### 1.**[口语] 竟然做某事；甚至做某事：**  

 > She goes so far as to declare that a Chinese word can never give you the exact meaning of a word in English!  
 > 她甚至说，汉语词绝对不能表达英语词的确切含义。  （2010 辽宁改）
