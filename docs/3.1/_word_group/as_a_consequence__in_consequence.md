### 1.**[正式用语] 结果；因此**  
英译 as a result：

 > It rained yesterday and as a consequence the match was canceled.  
 > 昨天的比赛因下雨而被取消。    
<audio src="./media/3-consequence.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > She was over the age limit and, in consequence, her application was rejected.  
 > 她超龄了，结果她的申请被拒绝了。
