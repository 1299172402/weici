英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **[正式用语] 停留；逗留 (stay)：**  

 > We were told to remain in our seats meanwhile with our seat belts fastened.   
 > 我们被告知系好安全带留在座位上。  （2010 四川）  
<audio src="./media/remain-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The rest of his family remained in Europe.    
 > 他其余的家人留在了欧洲。  （2009 湖北）  
<audio src="./media/remain-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **留存；剩下；遗留：**  

 > Education is what remains after one has forgotten everything he learned in school.  
 > 所谓教育，就是一个人把在学校所学的全部忘光后剩下的东西。  （——爱因斯坦）  
<audio src="./media/Education is what remains_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Very little of the house remained after the fire.   
 > 火灾后，房子所剩无几。    
<audio src="./media/remain-5.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 3.*高义频：* **[作系动词] 保持；仍然是：**  

- remain + adj:

 > **remain silent / seated**  
 > 保持沉默/留在座位上坐好    

 > I went to Professor Janye, who listened to my arguments but remained unmoved.  
 > 我去找了简妮教授，她听了我的理由但是仍无动于衷。  （2008 北京）  
<audio src="./media/I went to Professor Janye_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- remain + n:

 > In spite of their quarrel, they remain the best of friends.  
 > 尽管他们有争吵，他们仍然是最好的朋友。    
<audio src="./media/remain-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 4.*高义频：* **留待；尚待：**  

- remain to be done:

 > I never see what has been done; I only see what remains to be done.   
 > 我从来不看已经做了什么，我只看还有什么要做。  （人教④ – 1）  
<audio src="./media/remain-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- it remains to be seen + wh- 从句：

 > It remains to be seen whether you are right.  
 > 你是否正确，尚待分晓。    
<audio src="./media/remain-9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- there remains:

 > There remains a certain doubt among the people as to the practical value of the project.  
 > 关于这个项目的实用价值，人们还是有一些疑虑。  （2009 安徽）  
<audio src="./media/There remains a certain doubt among_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


派生词汇
---
remaining /rɪ'meɪnɪŋ/ adj   

真题解析
---
1. It was already past midnight and only three young men ________ (逗留) in the teahouse.  （2006 安徽改）  
2. It remains ________ (see) whether Jim’ll be fit enough to play in the finals.   （2006 浙江改）  

答案：
1. remained  2. to be seen
