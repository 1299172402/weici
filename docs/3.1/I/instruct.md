英文释义
---
### 1.*低义频：* **教授；指导**  
英译 to teach sb sth, or show sb how to do sth：

 > We should **instruct** the young not only **in** words, but by deeds.   
 > 我们应该对青年人言传身教。    
<audio src="./media/instruct-4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **指示；命令**  
英译 to officially tell sb to do sth (order)：

- instruct sb to do sth:

 > A computer can only do what you have instructed it to do.  
 > 电脑只会做你让它做的事。  （2009 重庆）  
<audio src="./media/A computer can only do _AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- instruct sb + wh- to do sth:

 > I have been instructed when to start.   
 > 我已得到何时出发的通知。    
<audio src="./media/instruct-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>
