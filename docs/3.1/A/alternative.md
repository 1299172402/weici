英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **[只用于名词前] 可供替代的；可供选择的**  
英译 that you can use, do, etc. instead of sth else：

 > an **alternative method** of learning  
 > 其他的学习方法    

 > The way was blocked, so we went by an alternative road.   
 > 这条路堵塞了，因此我们走了另一条路。    
<audio src="./media/3-alternative.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **非传统的；另类的**  
英译 different from what is usual or traditional ：

 > an **alternative lifestyle**  
 > 另类的生活方式

真题解析
---
1. The disappearance of dinosaurs is not necessarily caused by astronomical incidents. But_______explanations are hard to find.   （2017 江苏）  
A.alternative  
B.aggressive  
C.ambiguous  
D.apparent  
2. As there is less and less coal and oil, scientists are exploring new ways of making use of ________(替代能源), such as sunlight, wind and water for power and fuel.  （2009 湖北改）  

答案：
1. A  2. alternative energy
