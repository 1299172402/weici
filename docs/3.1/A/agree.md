英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **(意见、观点等) 一致**  
英译 to have the same opinion as sb/sth：
反义词 disagree 

 > — The service in the restaurant is terrible.   
 > 这家餐馆的服务真差劲。    
<audio src="./media/agree-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 >  — I **couldn’t agree more** / I **quite agree**.   
 > 我完全赞同。    

 > **It is** generally **agreed that** Beijing has a traffic problem.   
 > 人们普遍认为北京的交通有问题。    
<audio src="./media/agree-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Li Jiang and Su Hua don't **agree on** what to wear when greeting the British students.  
 > 李江和苏华在穿什么衣服去迎接英国学生的问题上意见不一。    
<audio src="./media/Li Jiang and Su Hua don't_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: agree on主要指双方通过协商而就某事取得一致意见或达成协议。  
### 2.*高义频：* **同意；赞同**  
英译 to say yes to sth：
反义词 refuse 

 > I've **agreed to** go with Alice to buy some clothes.  
 > 我已经答应和爱丽丝去买衣服。  （2017 全国）  
<audio src="./media/agree-101_AAC.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Make sure each group member understands and **agrees to** the task given to him or her.   
 > 确保每个小组成员都明白并且同意派给他或她的任务。  （2008 江苏）  
<audio src="./media/agree-6.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: agree to后面常接表示“提议”、“办法”、“计划”或“安排”等的词作宾语。  

固定搭配
---
## \#agree with
1.*高义频：* **同意；赞同：**  

 > agree with one’s idea / opinion   
 > 赞同某人的想法/意见    

 > I quite agree with him on the sports.   
 > 我非常同意他在运动方面的观点。    
<audio src="./media/agree-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: agree with表示“同意”、“赞同”时，后常接人或表示“意见”、“看法”的词作宾语。  
2.*高义频：* **与...相符；与...一致**  
英译 to be the same as sth：

 > His words do not agree with his actions.   
 > 他言行不一致。    
<audio src="./media/agree-8.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

3.*高义频：* **[常用于否定句] (食物等) 适合**  
英译 (of food, etc.) be suitable for sb：

 > Too much meat doesn’t agree with her.   
 > 她不宜吃太多肉。    
<audio src="./media/agree-10.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


派生词汇
---
agreeable  /ə'ɡriːəbl/ adj   

真题解析
---
1. You look well. The air and the sea foods in Sanya must agree ________ you, I suppose.  （2010 陕西改）  
2. — When did you last hear from Jay?
— He phoned me this morning, and we agreed ________ a time and place to meet.  （2008 湖南改）  

答案：
1. with  2. on
