### 1.*低义频：* **值得敬重的：**  

 > a worthy cause  
 > 崇高的事业    

 > a worthy champion   
 > 当之无愧的冠军    

### 2.*高义频：* **值得的：**  

- (be) worthy of sth:

 > she is a teacher who is worthy of respect  
 > 她是位值得尊敬的老师。    

 > He insisted that the problem worthy of attention be discussed at the meeting.  
 > 他坚持在会上讨论那个值得关注的问题。  （2010湖北改）
