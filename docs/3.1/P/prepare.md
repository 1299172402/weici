### 1.*高义频：* **使做好准备；把...准备好**  
英译 to make sth or sb ready to be used or to do sth：

 > prepare dinner  
 > 准备晚餐    

- prepare sth / sb for sb / sth:

 > A hotel room is being prepared for the guest.  
 > 正在为这位客人准备一间旅馆客房。    
<audio src="./media/prepare51.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The function of education is to prepare us for life.  
 > 教育的作用是让我们对生活有所准备。  （2011 安徽）  
<audio src="./media/prepare58.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

### 2.*高义频：* **(使自己) 有准备**  
英译 to make yourself ready to do sth or for sth that you expect to happen：

- prepare for sth:

 > Hope for the best, and prepare for the worst.  
 > 抱最好的希望，做最坏的打算。    
<audio src="./media/prepare53.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The lectures help students prepare for college mathematics.  
 > 这些课程有助于学生为大学数学做好准备。  （2012 北京改）  
<audio src="./media/prepare54.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prepare oneself (for sth):

 > I closed my eyes and prepared myself for his burst of anger.  
 > 我闭上眼睛做好了准备，等他大发雷霆。  （2013 陕西）  
<audio src="./media/prepare55.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- prepare (oneself) to do sth:

 > Buy the album, and prepare to be amazed.  
 > 买下这张专辑，准备好感受惊喜吧。    
<audio src="./media/prepare56.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > The troops prepared themselves to go into battle.  
 > 部队准备开赴战场。    
<audio src="./media/prepare57.aac" controls="controls"></audio>
