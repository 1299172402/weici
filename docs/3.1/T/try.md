英文释义
---
### 1.*高义频：* **尝试；试图；努力**  

 > **Try** your strength / **luck**.   
 > 试试你的力气/碰碰你的运气吧。    
<audio src="./media/try-1.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > **Try as she might** (= as hard as she could), Sue couldn’t get the door open.  
 > 苏想尽办法，但是仍开不开门。  （2011 全国）  
<audio src="./media/try4.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- try to do sth:

 > Try not to become a man of success but rather try to become a man of value.  
 > 不要为成功而努力，而要为做一个有价值的人而努力。  （—— 爱因斯坦）  
<audio src="./media/try-2.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

 > Bob **tried hard to** learn cooking, but the meals he prepared were terrible.  
 > 鲍勃非常努力地学习烹饪，但他做的饭菜很难吃。    
<audio src="./media/try-3.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- try one’s best (to do sth):

 > She never gave up but tried her best to overcome the difficulty in life.  
 > 她从未放弃过，而是努力克服生活中的困难。    
<audio src="./media/try7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

#### 辨析 manage, try
  
**manage**  强调结果，表示经过一番努力之后做成某事。
**try ** 强调动作，不表示结果如何。
 > I’ll try to get downtown this afternoon but I don’t know if I can make it.   
 > 今天下午我想去市中心，但不知是否能去成。    
<audio src="./media/manage-11.aac" controls="controls"></audio>


### 2.*高义频：* **试做；试用；试验：**  

 > If I’m going out for a meal, I prefer to **try something different**.   
 > 如果到外面吃饭，我喜欢尝试不同的饭菜。    
<audio src="./media/try-7.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

- try doing sth:

 > John isn’t here. Try phoning his home number.  
 > 约翰不在这里，试试打他家里的电话。    
<audio src="./media/try9.aac" controls="controls"></audio>

Notes: 1. try doing sth 一般表示“尝试做某事”，含 有试试看的意味；try to do sth 则表示“努力做某 事”。2. 口语中，尤其在祈使句中，常用try and 代替 try to。  
### 3.*低义频：* **考验；磨炼：**  

 > try sb’s courage   
 > 考验某人的胆量

真题解析
---
1. Susan wanted to be independent of her parents. She tried ________ (live) alone, but she didn’t like it and moved back home.   （2008 湖南改）  

答案：
1. living
